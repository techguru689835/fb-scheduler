# fb-scheduler

MadHub's powerful automation features enable you to streamline your social media management seamlessly. With the **Facebook Group Auto-Posting** feature, you can automatically execute posting tasks in multiple Facebook groups. This capability is ideal for marketers and community managers who aim to enhance their outreach and engagement effortlessly.

## Feature Overview

### Facebook Group Auto-Posting
MadHub's **Facebook Group Auto-Posting** feature automates the process of posting content across various groups. This powerful feature supports both input and file modes, allowing you to create posts using text input or load content from files. You can also set up content rotation and looping modes, ensuring your posts maintain freshness and relevance.

### Capabilities
- **Multiple Content Modes**: Choose between manual input or loading from files for versatile content management.
- **Content Rotation**: Automatically cycle through your posts to keep your audience engaged.
- **Loop Mode**: Repost content at set intervals to ensure continuous engagement.
- **Post Count Control**: Define how many times and how often your content is posted, giving you full control over your outreach strategies.

## Usage Instructions
To utilize the **Facebook Group Auto-Posting** feature effectively, follow these steps:

1. **Select the Groups**: Choose the Facebook groups where you want to post your content.
2. **Prepare Your Content**: You can enter your content manually or upload files that contain your posts.
3. **Configure Posting Parameters**:
   - **Single Group Post Count**: Set how many posts per group you want to make (e.g., 3 posts per group).
   - **Total Post Count**: Choose the total number of posts you want to execute across all groups (e.g., 30 posts in total).
   - **Content Rotation**: Activate to ensure different posts are showcased in a rotating manner.

### Configuration Example
To customize the **Facebook Group Auto-Posting** feature:
- Set your preferred **Post Count Per Group** to `5`, allowing for repeated engagement without overwhelming your audience.
- Utilize the **Total Post Count** setting and define it to `50` for comprehensive outreach.
- Activate **Loop Mode** if you wish to repeat your content every few days, making your management process even more efficient.
